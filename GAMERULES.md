# Rules:



# Players Turn:

You play a song, and then you guess the artist, song name, year released, and album. If you get it right or based on how close you were you deal more damage and build up combos. After you guess, it goes around in a circle and everyone else takes turns guessing if they want to, and then it reveals who is right. If the player whose turn it was gets at least the song name and the artist right, they deal damage to all other players, and if other players get any right they heal based on how many they got right.


# Game Format:

1. Pick which pack of songs you want to play. (can play multiple packs)
2. Randomize who goes first
3. First player gets a song and plays it for as long as they want, and then it goes into the guessing round
4. Player guesses, and then it goes around the circle with everyone else guessing
5. If the player gets all of them right, they deal max damage, otherwise the next person that got one right heals based on how right they were.
6. It keeps going around until there is only one person left with health.
